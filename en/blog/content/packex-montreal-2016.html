LM Manutentions will be at the PACKEX Montreal 2016

2016-11-07 - LM Manutentions

LM Manutentions invites you to meet its team of professionals at the booth 1601 of PACKEX Montreal 2016. The event, which will be held on November 30th and December 1st of 2016, is the most important exhibition for the packaging, handling and advanced manufacturing industries in Quebec.

Tell us about your projects!

Take advantage of this opportunity to discover our products and services and discuss your plans face-to-face with the competent and experienced advisers of LM Manutentions. Explore with them the most recent innovations in the sectors of conveyors, packaging, carriers, labels and labelling, automation and robotics in the handling of products that will showcase your projects.

More than 3,700 visitors

  • Executive Manager
  • Owner
  • Plant Engineer/Operations Maintenance
  • Packaging Designer
  • Manufacturer
  • Purchaser
  • Research & Development
  • Quality Control
  • Consultant

Five exhibitions in one

The advanced design and manufacturing event (ECFE) brings together under one roof the most prestigious exhibitions of advanced manufacturing in the region:  ACKEX, Expoplast, Automation Technology Expo (ATX), Design & Manufacturing, et Powder and Bulk Solids (PBS).

About LM Manutentions

Founded in 2001 by Caroline Lono, LM Manutentions is a company whose head office is located in the Laurentians, North of Montreal. We are expert advisors in the sale and installation of handling systems, conveyors, storage, packing and labelling to commercial or industrial companies.

Our mission is to offer you an optimal range of conveyorsof high quality at a competitive price. Our professionalism, customer service, post-sale support and the quality of our products are the secret of our success and a source of pride to us.

With recognized partners, we offer a full range of products and services, including a full Engineering Department as well as a team of installation and maintenance.

Whether you are looking for a simple conveyor, an automated system or a turnkey system, our experienced advisors are here to help you achieve your production and handling goals. LM Manutentions serves all regions of Quebec, Ontario and the Maritime Provinces.
